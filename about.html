About The Company

The Fons Vitae Publications is a publishing house. We publish books, e-books, history books, business books, science books, university students' research papers & journals and many other self help books.

The Project

This website is a research-based initiative titled "The Subjects of Al-Quran Al-Hakeem." Our goal is to provide user-friendly tools for searching, studying, reading, listening to, and exploring the Holy Quran in as many languages as possible. After an extensive research effort spanning from 2001 to 2025, we have identified and categorized more than 4,000 distinct subjects mentioned in the Holy Quran. These subjects cover a wide range of dimensions, including words, subjects, context, ideas, concepts, perception, and themes related to the past, present, and future. Our research delves into various aspects of life — Individual, Collective, History, Arts, Culture, Economic, Political, Social, and Spiritual — to provide a comprehensive understanding of the Holy Quran's guidance and relevance across times and cultures.

About Allah (God Almighty)

Allah—also known as God, Khuda, Malik, or Rabb—is the One and Only, the Creator of all that exists and all that does not. From absolute nothingness, Allah brought forth everything that was, is, and ever will be. He is eternal, without beginning or end, and beyond all limitations.

In the Holy Qur’an, Allah introduces Himself in many profound ways. One of the most well-known is:
"ٱلْـحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ"
“All praise is due to Allah, the Lord (Nurturer) of the Worlds.” (Surah Al-Fatiha 1:2)

He is also described as:
"مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ"
“Master of the Day of Judgment.” (Surah Al-Fatiha 1:4) — often pronounced as Ad-Deen, meaning the Day when all paths and choices will be judged.

These and many other divine names and attributes are revealed throughout Al-Qur’an Al-Hakeem (The Wise Qur’an), which we will explore in detail as we study the sacred text. Each name reflects an aspect of Allah’s infinite nature—His Mercy, Justice, Wisdom, and Sovereignty over all creation.

About Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him)

The Venerable, the Holiest of Holies, the one whose rank transcends all, the first addressee of the Holy Qur’an—Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) stands as the final Messenger in the divine chain that began with Adam (peace be upon him). He is the Seal of Prophethood, after whom no prophet shall come.

Allah Almighty has honored him with the highest status, describing him in the Holy Qur’an as:
"وَمَا أَرْسَلْنَاكَ إِلَّا رَحْمَةً لِّلْعَالَمِينَ"
“We have not sent you but as a mercy to the worlds.” (Surah Al-Anbiya 21:107)

This declaration highlights the universal compassion and divine mercy embodied in the life and message of the Prophet (PBUH). In our study, we will explore in depth how Allah has illuminated his noble character, his sublime attributes, and his pivotal role through various verses of the Holy Qur’an.

About the Holy Qur’an

Al-Qur’an Al-Hakeem—the Wise Qur’an—is one of the sacred revelations from Allah, the Almighty, the Most Merciful. It is the final and complete divine scripture, concluding the long chain of heavenly guidance that began with the creation of humanity and the life of Prophet Adam (peace be upon him) on Earth.

The Holy Qur’an represents the culmination and completion of Deen (the divinely ordained path), and the continuity of revelations that were sent to various prophets over time comes to its conclusion with this final book. It was revealed by Allah through His chosen angel, Jibreel (Gabriel), and was sent down upon the pure heart of the final Prophet, Muhammad (peace be upon him).

The Qur’an comprises approximately 77,439 words in its original Arabic text. It is divided into 114 chapters (Surahs) and contains 6,236 verses (Ayahs), offering timeless guidance for all of humanity until the end of time.

About Us

At Fons Vitae Publications, we are dedicated to making the Quran easier to navigate and understand—by compiling verses by subject for quick reference and deeper insight. This unique research is the personal work of Kamran Ahmed Bahalim, who, driven by a sincere desire to understand the Quran holistically, spent years organizing its messages by topic.

Whether you’re curious about what the Quran says about clouds, agriculture, or any other theme, our platform brings all related verses together—making study easier and more meaningful.

This is a completely independent project, not affiliated with any group or sect—created purely out of love for humanity and the guidance of Allah. We welcome all languages, all people, and all feedback.
